### Why Learn it?
Learning how to develop a neural network from scratch can teach you the mathematical background and algorithmic logic behind particular model types. In this repository we will be focusing on what's underneath a convolutional neural network. Convolutional neural networks (CNN) is excellent at classifying images compared to other models. CNNs are capable of identifying image patterns through a special layer called the convolutional layer which gives a decimal score of what class the image may be. It is important to learn in-depth about CNNs as it doesn't only provide a foundational understanding of how neural networks work but it also enables us to use the model for many different use cases such as pose detection, object detection, and image segmentation. 

### What do you need to know before starting?
Whilst there is no need for an individual to know anything about the CNN model, it would still be immensely helpful if they knew how the basic architecture of a CNN model and its processes through the usage of external libraries such as PyTorch, Keras, and TensorFlow. These libraries provide prebuilt libraries that eliminates the need to know the complex formulas of a CNN model allowing for easy development of models. ### How useful is this skill, compared with the effort of learning it?
Once you already know how to develop a CNN model with prebuilt libraries, it becomes a matter of defining each layer manually and writing down the mathematical equations used to calculate each parameters. By developing a neural network from scratch, it enables us to understand the fundamentals of deep learning and see how mathematics is used in the model. This way, it becomes possible to develop new methods of approaching particular neural network tasks as it becomes highly customisable.
